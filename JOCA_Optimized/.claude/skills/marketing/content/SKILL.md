## Content Strategy (planning what to create)

GATHER: business goal (traffic/leads/brand/thought leadership) · ICP + their questions before buying · objections in sales calls · support ticket language · existing content + what works · formats available · competitors' gaps

CONTENT ARCHITECTURE:
- Pillar pages (broad topic, ≥2000 words, link hub) → cluster posts (specific subtopics, link back to pillar)
- Keyword intent: informational (awareness) · navigational (consideration) · commercial (decision) · transactional (action)
- Mix ratio: 70% SEO-targeted · 20% thought leadership · 10% product-focused

EDITORIAL CALENDAR: month 1-2 (foundational pillars) → month 3-4 (cluster posts) → month 5-6 (conversion content + repurposing)

## Copywriting (persuasion + conversion)

PRINCIPLES: clarity > cleverness · benefits > features (feature: what it does; benefit: what that means for customer) · specificity > vagueness ("Cut reporting from 4h to 15min" not "Save time") · customer language (mirror their words from reviews/interviews) · one idea per section

FRAMEWORKS:
- PAS: Problem → Agitate → Solution
- BAB: Before → After → Bridge
- AIDA: Attention → Interest → Desire → Action

HEADLINE FORMULA: [Specific outcome] + [time frame or ease qualifier] + [objection handler]
CTA: specific verb + benefit ("Start free trial" not "Submit")

PAGE STRUCTURE: Hook (what's in it for them, ≤8 words) → Problem → Solution → Social proof → Features as benefits → Objection handling → CTA

## Social Media

PLATFORM DEFAULTS: LinkedIn (professional insights, data, career) · Twitter/X (hot takes, threads, news) · Instagram (visual, behind-the-scenes, carousels) · TikTok (entertainment + education, hooks ≤3s) · Shorts/Reels (repurpose long-form)

HOOKS (first line): question · bold statement · "I [did X] and [surprising result]" · "Most [group] don't know..." · number ("5 things...")

SHORT-FORM VIDEO SCRIPT: Hook (0-3s) → Problem/Setup (3-10s) → Solution/Value (10-40s) → CTA (last 5s)

REPURPOSING: blog post → LinkedIn carousel + Twitter thread + TikTok script + email · podcast → quote graphics + key takeaway posts

ENFORCE: customer language not company language · one primary CTA per piece · check product-marketing-context before asking questions

NEVER: vague benefits ("improve your workflow") · jargon customers don't use · bury the lead · one-size-fits-all across platforms
